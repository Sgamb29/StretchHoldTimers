About The Stretch Hold Timers Site

Home

A collection of timers that make it easy to time how long to hold stretches for. No need to click to silence an alarm, simply press one button depending on the duration you would like when you're ready to start a stretch. Displays the stopwatch countdown of time left in the stretch and indicates the finish with a short sound and a color change.
